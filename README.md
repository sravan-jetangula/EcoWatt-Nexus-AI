# 🌱 EcoWatt Nexus AI

### Smart Home Energy Forecasting & Intelligent Load Optimization Platform

**EcoWatt Nexus AI** is an AI-powered sustainability platform designed to help households understand energy consumption patterns, forecast electricity demand, and optimize appliance usage for improved efficiency and reduced carbon emissions.

The project aligns with the **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**:

* ⚡ **SDG 7** — Affordable and Clean Energy
* 🌍 **SDG 13** — Climate Action

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

EcoWatt Nexus AI simulates an intelligent energy management ecosystem where users enter their **Electricity Meter / Consumer Number** and receive:

* Energy consumption forecasting
* Grid stress analysis
* Smart appliance optimization recommendations
* Estimated electricity bill savings
* Carbon emission reduction insights
* Responsible AI transparency reports

The platform demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can support sustainable energy consumption and environmental conservation.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many households are unaware of:

* Peak energy consumption periods
* Grid stress conditions
* Energy-efficient appliance scheduling
* Environmental impact of electricity usage

This lack of visibility often results in:

* Higher electricity bills
* Increased carbon emissions
* Poor utilization of renewable energy resources
* Additional pressure on local power grids

EcoWatt Nexus AI addresses these challenges through AI-driven forecasting and optimization.

---

## 💡 Key Features

### 🔢 Consumer ID Based Analysis

Users provide an electricity meter number that acts as a secure identifier for generating personalized energy insights.

### 🌡 Weather-Aware Energy Simulation

The platform incorporates environmental conditions such as:

* Ambient temperature
* Seasonal load variations
* Weather-driven consumption patterns

### ⚠️ Grid Stress Prediction

Identifies periods of high electricity demand and potential grid stress conditions.

### 💡 Smart Load Optimization

Provides actionable recommendations such as:

* Shifting appliance usage to off-peak hours
* Utilizing solar-friendly time windows
* Reducing unnecessary energy consumption

### 🌱 Sustainability Metrics

Calculates:

* Estimated bill savings
* Carbon emission reductions
* Energy efficiency improvements

### 🔒 Responsible AI Framework

Protects user privacy through:

* Simulated data processing
* Anonymous consumption trend analysis
* No storage of personal information

## 🐳 Docker Deployment

### Build Docker Image

```bash
docker build -t ecowatt-nexus-ai .
```

### Run Container

```bash
docker run -p 8501:8501 ecowatt-nexus-ai
```

Application URL:

```text
http://localhost:8501
```
